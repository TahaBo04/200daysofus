200 Days of Us

Not all of them were easy.
Not all of them were light.
But every single one of them, I chose you.

An honest pause

Because pretending everything was perfect would be a lie.

We went through misunderstandings.
Moments where words hurt.
Moments where silence hurt more.

There were times I reacted instead of listening. Times I spoke without understanding. Times I should have been softer.

I see that now.

This is me being honest

I’m sorry for the moments I made you feel unheard. Sorry for the weight you carried when I should have helped lift it. Sorry for the confusion when I should have brought clarity.

I never wanted to be the source of your pain. And when I was — even unintentionally — I take responsibility.

You deserved patience. You deserved reassurance. You deserved love that felt safe.

— Still learning. Still choosing you.

Why I still choose you

  • Because love isn’t proven on easy days.
  • Because staying matters more than winning.
  • Because even after the arguments, you’re still home.
  • Because 200 days later — it’s still you.

I don’t promise perfection.
I promise effort.

I promise to listen more.
To react less.
To love you the way you deserve — not the way I find easiest.

200 days in.
Still choosing you.
